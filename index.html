What Do You know?

The game that tests what you know?

"What Do You Know?" is a two-player modern trivia game that tests your knowledge on various topics.


Here's how it works:


  1. Players can select any of the provided categories. The number on each box represents how much the question under that category is worth
  2. After the player selects a category, they will have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as they can. After one minute, players can declare a winnder (the player with the most correct answers) or continue playing the game
  3. If answered correctly, the player will receive the amount of points the question was worth. If the answered incorrectly, the player will not earn any points.
  4. Players will have a total of 30 seconds to answer as many questions as they can. The player with the most points after 30 seconds is the winner
